Who we are

The Ateneo Registration Committee (RegCom) is a body of selected student volunteers who are tasked by the Registrar’s Office (RO) to enforce and facilitate the registration process and assist all academic departments, administrative offices, personnel and students involved in it.

RegCom is responsible for producing and disseminating documents, reminders, and promotions pertaining to enlistment and the registration process. They also facilitate the flow of manual registration with the help of the Loyola Schools Assessment Team (LSAT).
