Additional Reminders and Announcements:
  • Be informed that you can print your assessment forms at home and proceed directly to the Cashier for tuition payment. Should you wish to make further changes in your enlistment and assessment, proceed to the enlistment venues (during regular registration).

  • Starting SY 2008-2009 registration forms will no longer be distributed except for special cases such as students who are readmitted or are returning from leave of absence (LOA)

  • Starting SY 2008-2009 grade reports will no longer be distributed. Students may view their grade reports in their AISIS account page. Parents' copy of grade reports will be mailed out as usual.
